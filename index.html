Computer Gaming Industry Data from 1980 to 2016

Data Visulaization Project with JS and D3
Kristine Cheng

This visualization is an interactive slideshow that drive users around to explore Sales Data for the Computer Gaming Industry.
The dataset itself contains various game information including publisher/developer, revenue for different region, year published, and genre.

Overview of the dataset

Scene 1

At this scene, you could find changes through 1980 to 2016 years in different fields in different games.
Users are able to choose different genres to view the revenue, and can also slide the year bar to explore the growth of different game genres.
